Part of being a musician for me is the diversity of it all; going from pop one moment to jazz the next. For that reason I play in many different groups and constellations. Here is a selection of a few of my favorite ones.
In 2016, after the release tour of his album "Amygdala", Tingsek decided to record a live studio video of three of his tunes, which I was thrilled to be a part of. Released later as an EP. This one is called Let That Go.
Shirin and I have been playing together since 2014, and she is a dear friend of mine. She isn't 22 anymore, but her song about an older lover keeps the nostalgia at its most. Starring me on acoustic piano.
Here it is, John Venkiah Trio's third album. We recorded it in 2020, celebrating ten years together. This album is more inspired by my soul and R'n'B influences than the previous releases. Starring special guests Cosima Lamberth on vocals and Karl-Martin Almqvist on tenor saxophone.
Faela is the craziest, most colourful gang of musicians you can think of. With a singer from Argentina, and roots from all over the world, Faela can make an old tree dance. I have been part of this group since 2015, touring around Europe. This record was recorded in 2016 and has some amazing tunes.
SaliBambra is a mix of jazz and crazy. Seven members, each with their own flavour. On this album from 2017, Cristoffer Csanady (double bass) had the idea to create songs about different stages in life, in Swedish. All of us composed a tune or two each.
Elevation, by John Venkiah Trio was released in 2017, and contains original pieces written by me, some with vocals and some without. We performed a release tour playing at places like Fasching, Stockholm and Montmartre, Copenhagen.
Helena Josefsson, backing vocalist with Per Gessle (most known from the group Roxette), but has som fantastic music of her own. I am honored to be a part of this album, which took place in a studio in the middle of the woods in south Sweden, sleeping in a haunted house!
Per-Oscar Nilsson is an amazing guitarist and composer resident in Malmö. Sweden. This album, from 2013, contains originals and oozes both calmness and chaos.
